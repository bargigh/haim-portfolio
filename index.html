About Haim

Creating moving and still images has been my intense passion for over 20 years. Born in Israel, I established my career in Tel Aviv, worked across Europe, and in 2001 settled in New York City — where I joined a premier creative production company producing broadcast commercials, digital content, and short documentary films for some of the most prestigious brands worldwide.

I worked in studio and on-location as DP, camera operator, and data manager, and was integrally involved in storyboard creation, research, and web design.

I moved back to Israel in 2019 to reestablish myself in Tel Aviv. I have a 24/7 work ethic and I'm not happy unless I'm generating moving, meaningful, uplifting images on a daily basis.
